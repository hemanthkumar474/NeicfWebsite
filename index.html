ABOUT NEICF

NEICF is a telugu fellowship in New England area. We gather every fourth Saturday. We have an all night prayer on the second friday each month, in the home which is open for the all night prayer. We pray for the family which opened its home, pray for all our fellowship families, pray for all prayer requests that we have received.
