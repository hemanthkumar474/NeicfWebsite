Prayer Drive 2018 - Across State of Massachusetts

Jonah 4:11 - "And should I not have concern for the great city of Nineveh, in which there are more than a hundred and twenty thousand people who cannot tell their right hand from their left.. "

The prayer drive is based on biblical principles in Eze 22:30; Gen 13:17; 2 Chr 7:14; 1 Tim 2:1-4

2018'Prayer Dive is call to all men and women of God to join us in prayer and intercede for the state of MA.

We believe the solutions to the world’s problems lie not in human intellect or power but in the grace and intervention of the Almighty

We would like to meet with local Christians in any of these cities, intercede in prayer for the people and the authorities

- NEICF Prayer Team
